Design Philosophy

Born from a Simple Belief:
No Thought Should Be Lost

We've all been there — a brilliant idea strikes while you're in a meeting, cooking, or falling asleep. By the time you find the right app, open it, choose a folder... the thought is gone.

QuickNote AI was built on the principle of zero-friction capture. Press one hotkey, type your thought in plain language, and let AI do the organizing. Whether it's a reminder ("call Mom in 30 minutes"), a task ("fix the login bug"), or a random spark ("what if we added dark mode?") — QuickNote AI understands intent and acts accordingly.

Follow your heart, capture every moment — that's not just a tagline. It's how we think about productivity: it should serve you, not the other way around.

Pro Tips for Power Users

Get the most out of QuickNote AI with these quick tricks

⌨️

Master the Hotkey

Default is ⌘+Shift+N — works from any app. Customize it in Settings to your preferred shortcut. The input box appears as an overlay, so you never lose context.

🗣️

Talk to It Like a Human

Don't overthink formatting. Type remind me to call dentist tomorrow at 3pm or idea: what if we made the logo animated? — AI understands natural language and intent.

📋

Check Your Daily Report

Every evening, QuickNote generates a summary of your day — completed tasks, pending items, and AI insights. Find it in the Reports tab on the sidebar.

🔗

Paste Links, AI Does the Rest

Paste a URL and QuickNote automatically classifies it as a Link with relevant tags. Great for bookmarking articles, repos, or videos for later.
